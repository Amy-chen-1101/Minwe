Our Company

Minwe is a leading electronic device manufacturer in China. We focus on the research, development, production, and sales of high-quality power solutions, including smart battery chargers, portable power supplies, and jump starters.

With 25 years of industry experience, we have established a strong R & D team and production base to meet the needs of global customers. Our products not only lead the domestic market but are also exported to many countries and regions in Europe, America, and Asia.

We always adhere to the business philosophy of "quality first, customer first" and are committed to providing customers with the best products and services. Through continuous innovation and improvement, we have become one of the leading enterprises in the electronic device industry.
